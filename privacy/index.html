Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

WhosNext ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

WhosNext is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Analytics Data: We use Aptabase, a privacy-friendly analytics service, to collect anonymous usage statistics. This includes app launches, feature usage counts, and general device information (OS version, device type). This data is fully anonymized and cannot be used to identify you.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information through the app.
  • No Location Data: We do not access or store your location.
  • No Account Required: WhosNext does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Local Storage

WhosNext stores your preferences (such as selected theme and language) locally on your device using shared preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

  • Aptabase: Privacy-focused analytics. Aptabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: The app is distributed through Apple's App Store, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

WhosNext does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal data to function.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The anonymous analytics data collected by Aptabase is used solely to improve the app experience.

Your Rights

Since we collect only anonymous analytics data and store preferences locally on your device, you have full control over your data:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences.
  • You can contact us to request information about what anonymous data has been collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
